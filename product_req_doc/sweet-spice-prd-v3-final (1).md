### Design Principles
- Never reveal what the right answer looks like
- Tricky options that sound correct but signal wrong energy
- Paradox questions where the pattern across multiple answers matters more than any single answer
- Long text evaluated for self-awareness, honesty, emotional specificity — how she answers, not just what
- One question at a time with progress bar
- Cannot go back and change — answers are final
- Closing space for her to express vibe interest — optional, not scored, seen by Ahmad

- I ask for it directly — I do not do hints

The evaluation prompt in Section 11 works as-is with Grok. Send it as the system message, her formatted answers as the user message. Use low temperature (0.3) for consistent scoring across submissions.

```javascript
const response = await fetch("https://api.x.ai/v1/chat/completions", {
  method: "POST",
  headers: {
    "Content-Type": "application/json",
    "Authorization": `Bearer ${GROK_API_KEY}`
  },
  body: JSON.stringify({
    model: "grok-2-latest",
    messages: [
      { role: "system", content: EVALUATION_PROMPT },

});
```
